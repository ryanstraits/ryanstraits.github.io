my life now sits in boxes in the corners of my room….

the majority of the “big move” is over…the brunt of the storm has passed…now we’ve just gotta organize and clean up the aftermath…as for me i need to find some new furniture for my room…and i gotta get used to these cotton pickin’ new sheets…i hate new sheets…all hard and rough like…but unfortunately it’s fashion over function with my mom…aka the home designer nazi…

but alas life is good in the new digs…it’s been a long, arduous weekend…but things are falling into place…and i’m thankful for my new home…

—

and now let the random musings begin…

i saw the first big blockbuster of the summer friday nite…and it has gotten me very pumped for this whole coming movie season…that’s right…i saw episode three…and i have now determined that this whole star wars phenomena is so much more than mere movies could ever contain…they are epic in scope…mythic in structure…and just plain mesmerizing to behold…i need to see them all again…and that right soon…

the lost season finale is this wed. nite…i shall be blocking off the entire evening for the event…it’s going to be so good that they had to make it two hours in length…either that or they had to make it that long to compete with the two hr. finale of american idol…if so, then i now have muchos gratitude for network competition…

tomorrow morning at roughly 10AM i will have in my possession four tickets to see coldplay utterly annihilate everyone in attendance at the post-gazette pavilion in pittsburgh this coming august…that’s right four tickets…and there’s only one me…so let the bribes and buttering up begin…

sidenote: let’s all give a hardy “amen” to memorial day weekend…oh, the sweet bliss of a paid holiday…

i have finally gotten new batteries for my digital camera so expect many new pictures on this site soon…this will most likely include a new site design and layout…gasp…

hold onto your nates folks…cuz this summer’s gonna kick arse…

—

it’s just about time to go home…and so the rain begins to pour…
